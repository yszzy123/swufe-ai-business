Ling Liu

Professor & Doctoral Supervisor
Biography

Dr. Ling Liu is a Professor and Doctoral Supervisor at SWUFE. She obtained her Ph.D. from Durham University, UK. Her research interests primarily lie in Financial Intelligence, Text Analysis, and Social Media Analysis. She has presided over multiple prestigious research projects, including grants from the National Natural Science Foundation of China (NSFC).
