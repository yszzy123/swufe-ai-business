Chuan Luo

Professor & Doctoral Supervisor
Biography

Dr. Chuan Luo is a Professor and Doctoral Supervisor at the School of Management Science and Engineering, Southwestern University of Finance and Economics (SWUFE). He formerly served as a Senior Research Associate at City University of Hong Kong.

Professor Luo is a distinguished scholar in the field of Information Systems. He serves as an expert consultant for the Sichuan Provincial Department of Commerce and the Chengdu Municipal Commission of Commerce. He is an Executive Committee Member of the Association for Information Systems (AIS) Asia-Pacific Chapter. He also serves as a reviewer for numerous prestigious SCI/SSCI journals including JAIS, I&M, DSS, and IJEC. He has chaired tracks at major international conferences such as PACIS and AMCIS.
