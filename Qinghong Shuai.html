Qinghong Shuai

Professor & Doctoral Supervisor
Biography

Dr. Qinghong Shuai is a Professor and Doctoral Supervisor at SWUFE. He serves as the Director of the Institute of Internet Finance and Payment. He is a member of the Teaching Steering Committee for E-commerce Majors (Ministry of Education) and a special researcher for the Payment and Clearing Center of the People's Bank of China. He has published nearly 20 books and 40 academic papers on FinTech, Electronic Payment, and Digital Economy.
