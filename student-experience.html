The Hub: AI Business Elite Club

To bridge the gap between academic theory and business practice, the Department of E-commerce established the "AI Business Elite Club". This club serves as the primary practical platform for our major. Open to students across the university, it fosters interdisciplinary collaboration, allowing you to form diverse teams and tackle real-world challenges through competitions, projects, and research from day one.

Insight

Conducting AI Application Lectures

We regularly invite renowned experts from both academia and industry to share the latest frontiers in Artificial Intelligence. Our lecture series covers critical topics such as Generative AI Applications, Agentic Commerce, and the Consumer Metaverse, ensuring our students' knowledge stays in sync with rapid technological evolution.

Leadership

Hosting AI Application Competitions

Adhering to the philosophy of "Promoting learning and teaching through competitions", we provide a stage for students to unleash their creativity. We regularly host high-level challenges, including the "Intelligent Assistant Virtual Avatar Design Competition", the 1st Sichuan "AI Agent+" Innovation Design Competition, and the campus rounds of national events like the "Three Innovation" Challenge, fostering a vibrant atmosphere of innovation.

Field Research

Surveying AI Application Enterprises

We believe classrooms have no walls. Through our "Journey to Intelligence" program, we lead students into top tech enterprises to understand the business logic behind AI. From attending the Amazon Web Services (AWS) Summit to conducting field research at industry leaders like Mingtu Tech, Guxiaozhi, and the Chengdu AI Digital Trade Center, students gain first-hand insight into the future of intelligent commerce.
