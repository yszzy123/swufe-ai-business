Biography

Dr. Qian Liang is a Lecturer at the School of Management Science and Engineering, Southwestern University of Finance and Economics (SWUFE). She joined SWUFE in 2021.

She received her Ph.D. from Xi'an Jiaotong University and was a joint Ph.D. student at the University of Pittsburgh. Her research interests focus on decision analysis in social networks and user behavior modeling. She has published research papers in Knowledge-Based Systems and Computers & Industrial Engineering.
